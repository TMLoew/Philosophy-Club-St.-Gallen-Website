What we stand for

The club connects students from different backgrounds who like to argue, listen and rethink their assumptions – in a friendly, low‑threshold environment.

Various Opinions

We represent a diverse society that is rich in ideas and enjoys the interaction with people who think differently. Hopefully you want to be a part of that!

Insightful Events

During the semester we organise events to talk about specific philosophers, interesting problems or invite guest speakers to share their perspective.

Binding interactions

Through exciting internal events such as cooking nights, film screenings or visiting the Christmas market in St. Gallen we forge close connections.
